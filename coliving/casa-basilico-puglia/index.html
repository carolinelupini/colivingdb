Where to stay in Puglia

  • Ostuni — hilltop white-washed old town, scenic, station outside town
  • Lecce — baroque city, walkable, best train connections, car-free base
  • Polignano a Mare — cliffside, touristy in summer, on the main train line
  • Monopoli — working port town, quieter, well connected by train

Getting around

The Bari-Brindisi-Lecce train line connects the main towns and each center is walkable, but a car is worth renting for masserie, beaches, and inland villages.

Getting there

  • Brindisi Airport (BDS) ~51 kmNearest airport · nonstop routes →
  • Rome–Fiumicino Leonardo da Vinci International Airport (FCO) ~443 kmMajor international hub · nonstop routes →

Bari Airport is nearest, reached by shuttle (~30 min, €5) or train (~20 min, €5.30) to Bari Centrale, then onward transport to Putignano.
