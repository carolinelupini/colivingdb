Where to stay in Encinitas

  • Downtown / 101 Corridor — surf shops, cafes, walkable strip, most central
  • Cardiff-by-the-Sea — quieter beach town, laid-back, still walkable to surf
  • Leucadia — bohemian, funky, low-key, good coffee shops
  • Olivenhain — inland, rural/equestrian, quiet, needs a car

Getting around

A car is standard; the Coaster commuter train runs along the coast to San Diego; each town center is walkable but you'll drive between them; San Diego airport is 30-40 minutes.
