Where to stay in Sofia

  • Tsentar (Center) — historic core, walkable, cafes and coworking
  • Lozenets — trendy residential, expat-popular, near South Park
  • Ivan Vazov — quiet, central-adjacent, mostly residential
  • Krastova Vada — near Vitosha mountain access, quieter, less touristy

Getting around

Cheap and efficient metro, trams, and buses; the center is walkable; airport to center is about 20 minutes on the direct metro line.
