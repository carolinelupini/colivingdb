Things to do nearby

General Luna is Siargao's main hub and Cloud 9's home base — where surf schools, coworking cafés, and most of the island's nomad and surfer population actually live, versus the island's more scattered outer barangays.

Where to stay in General Luna (Siargao)

  • Tourism Road / Back Road — restaurant and bar strip, most accommodation, nomad hub
  • Cloud 9 — surf-break area, small cluster of stays, best for dedicated surfers
  • Santa Fe — relaxed, residential, still local but growing with nomads

Getting around

A scooter is the standard way to get around General Luna and the island; Sayak (Siargao) airport to town is about 45-60 minutes by van or tricycle, with limited paved roads.
