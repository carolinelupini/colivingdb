Where to stay in Vilnius

  • Užupis — bohemian arts district, cafes, self-declared "republic"
  • Senamiestis (Old Town) — historic, walkable, UNESCO-listed
  • Naujamiestis — central business and residential mix
  • Žvėrynas — leafy, quiet, riverside residential

Getting around

Compact and walkable center with a solid bus/trolleybus network; airport to center is about 10 minutes by bus or taxi, one of Europe's closest capital airports.
