Things to do nearby

Kotor draws nomads for dramatic bay scenery, a walkable medieval old town, and Balkan cost of living with the euro as currency; cruise-ship crowds fill the old town by midday.

  • Climb the Kotor City Walls
  • Old Town wander
  • Boat to Our Lady of the Rocks
  • Drive Lovćen National Park
  • Day trip to Budva

UNESCO World Heritage nearby: Natural and Culturo-Historical Region of Kotor ~1 km · Old city (Dubrovnik) ~59 km · Vjetrenica ~80 km

National parks: Lovćen National Park ~4 km · Skadar Lake National Park ~41 km

Eat: Try njeguški pršut (smoked ham), fish soup (riblja čorba), and cevapi.
