Where to stay in Puerto Escondido

  • Zicatela — surf-central, bars and restaurants, most coworking-friendly
  • La Punta — bohemian, yoga and vegan cafes, popular with remote workers
  • Rinconada — upscale and residential, near quieter Carrizalillo beach
  • Bacocho — low-density, quiet, further from the main strip

Getting around

Walkable within Zicatela/La Punta but tuk-tuks and taxis cover longer hops; no metro; the airport is a 10-15 minute ride from most beach neighborhoods.
