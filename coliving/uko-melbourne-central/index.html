Where to stay in Melbourne

  • Fitzroy — bohemian, cafes, arts, popular with creatives
  • CBD — central transit hub, apartments, walkable grid
  • St Kilda — beachside, laid-back, cafes and nightlife
  • Collingwood/Richmond — trendy, close to CBD, good value

Getting around

Excellent tram network plus trains and buses; walkable within inner neighborhoods; no direct airport train yet (under construction), so SkyBus to the CBD takes 25-30 minutes.
