Where to stay in Anjuna, Goa

  • Anjuna — flea market, party legacy, mixed nomad/tourist crowd
  • Assagao — inland village, quiet, boutique cafes with fiber internet
  • Vagator — red cliffs, sunset cafes, coworking option, short scooter ride
  • Ashwem — beachfront, quieter, coworking with sunset views

Getting around

A rented scooter is the standard way to get around narrow village roads; no reliable public transit; Dabolim/Goa airport is 45-75 minutes depending on traffic.
