Things to do nearby

Zanzibar City (Stone Town) is the island's historic, walkable urban base - a UNESCO old town, port access, and the most infrastructure of anywhere on the island, though the swimmable beaches are elsewhere.

Where to stay in Zanzibar City

  • Stone Town — historic UNESCO core, walkable, cafes and coworking
  • Shangani — upscale part of Stone Town, seafront, restaurants
  • Kiponda — old town, budget guesthouses, narrow lanes
  • Ng'ambo — local, non-touristy, everyday city life

Getting around

Stone Town's narrow alleys are walkable and largely car-free; taxis and dala-dalas cover the wider city; the airport is about 15 minutes away.
