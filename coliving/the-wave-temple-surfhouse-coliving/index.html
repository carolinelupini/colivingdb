Where to stay in Las Palmas de Gran Canaria

  • Las Canteras — beachfront promenade, cafes and coworking, the nomad hub
  • Vegueta — historic old town, atmospheric streets, slower pace
  • Triana — central shopping street, walkable, local-tourist mix
  • Ciudad Jardín — leafy and quiet, upscale residential, still central

Getting around

Very walkable along the Las Canteras beach promenade; city buses (guaguas) cover the rest, no car needed; airport is 25-30 minutes away by bus or taxi.
