Things to do nearby

Arusha draws remote workers and safari-goers as East Africa's safari gateway — jumping-off point for the northern circuit parks — with a cooler climate, a genuine expat/NGO community, and lower costs than Nairobi.

Where to stay in Arusha

  • Njiro — top expat pick, good internet, restaurants and gym, 10 min from center
  • Sakina — residential, quieter, popular with longer-term expats

Getting around

No formal public transit system; daladalas (shared minibuses) and boda-bodas cover local trips, but most expats use a car or taxi; Kilimanjaro International Airport to Arusha is about 45-60 minutes.
