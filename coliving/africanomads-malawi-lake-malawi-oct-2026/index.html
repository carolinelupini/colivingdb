Things to do nearby

Freshwater lake towns like Cape Maclear and Nkhata Bay draw a small but steady backpacker-nomad crowd for cheap lakeside living, diving among the rift lake's cichlid fish, and a slow pace far from East Africa's coastal hubs.

◆HighlightsSnorkel or dive Lake Malawi's cichlidsCape Maclear beach at ChembeBoat trip to Mumbo IslandLivingstonia mission hikeNkhata Bay dive school
⇢Day tripsThumbi Island snorkel and kayak trip, ~30 min by boatDomwe Island, ~30 min by boatMushroom Farm and waterfalls near Livingstonia, ~2 hrNyika National Park, ~3 hr

●Eat: Try chambo (Lake Malawi's signature fish), nsima with beans, and fresh grilled fish off the beach.

Getting around

No real public transport network along the lakeshore; getting between lake towns typically means a private transfer, shared minibus (matola), or ferry over rough roads.
