Where to stay in Florianópolis

  • Lagoa da Conceição — lakeside nomad hub, cafes, coworking, nightlife
  • Centro — downtown transit hub, historic, practical base
  • Jurerê Internacional — upscale beach enclave, quieter, resort feel
  • Canasvieiras — budget-friendly north-shore beach, more local

Getting around

An island city best covered by car or scooter since buses between beaches are slow and infrequent; Uber is widely used; airport is 20-30 minutes from Lagoa/Centro.
