Where to stay in Saligao, Goa

  • Saligao — Quiet village vibe with walking trails, near Calangute
  • Assagao — Quieter still, boutique cafes and coworking, favored by focused workers
  • Anjuna — Livelier and bohemian, best cafe and nightlife density

Getting around

No reliable public transit — a scooter is standard for North Goa; Goa's airport to Saligao is roughly 45-75 minutes by car depending on which airport.
