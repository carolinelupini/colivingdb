## About

All-inclusive 3-night adult summer camp ~1hr from Cape Town: houses, tiny homes, Airstreams and glamping tents; meals, open bar and all activities included. Rotates location yearly (past: Austria, Serbia, Mexico, France). Ticket is per event, not per month.
