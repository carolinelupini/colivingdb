Where to stay in Jaipur

  • C-Scheme — central, corporate vibe, fine dining, close to business hubs
  • Malviya Nagar — startup/coworking hub, young crowd, near the airport

Getting around

Auto-rickshaws, Ola/Uber, and a growing metro line cover the city; a car or rickshaw is the norm since walkability is limited by traffic and heat; Jaipur airport to Malviya Nagar is about 15-20 minutes.
