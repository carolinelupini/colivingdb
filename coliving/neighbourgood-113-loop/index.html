Where to stay in Cape Town

  • Sea Point — beachfront promenade, cafes, walkable, popular with remote workers
  • City Bowl / CBD — central, restaurants and coworking, close to Table Mountain
  • Woodstock — creative, arty, up-and-coming, good coffee shops
  • Observatory — student-y, budget-friendly, bars and live music

Getting around

No metro; Uber/Bolt or a rental car are the norm, MyCiTi buses cover some routes, airport to center is 20-30 minutes.
