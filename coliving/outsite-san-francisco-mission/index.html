Where to stay in San Francisco

  • Mission District — colorful, cafes, younger creative crowd, very walkable
  • Hayes Valley — boutique shops, central, walkable, close to transit
  • SoMa — tech offices and coworking spaces, modern high-rises
  • Nob Hill / Russian Hill — quieter, residential, hilly, cable car access

Getting around

SF is compact with a solid Muni/BART network; a car isn't necessary in most neighborhoods; SFO to downtown is 30-45 minutes via BART or car.
