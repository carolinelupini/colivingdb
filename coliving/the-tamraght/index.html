Things to do nearby

Agadir offers a more modern, rebuilt Moroccan coastal base than Marrakech's medina chaos - a wide beach promenade, reliable sun, and surf towns like Taghazout just up the coast for weekend breaks.

Where to stay in Agadir

  • Talborjt — authentic, local souks, affordable, less touristy
  • Founty — beachfront, upscale resorts, relaxed pace, cafes
  • Secteur Balnéaire — central hotel and marina zone, promenade, walkable

Getting around

Compact beachfront core is walkable; petit taxis and ride-hailing cover the rest; airport (Al Massira) is about 30-40 minutes from the city center.
