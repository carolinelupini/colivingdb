Where to stay in Valencia

  • Ruzafa (Russafa) — Hip cafes and coworking, the default nomad base
  • El Carmen — Historic old-town charm, dense nightlife, touristy
  • Cabanyal — Beachside, up-and-coming, quieter artsy fishing quarter

Getting around

Flat and highly bikeable with dedicated lanes citywide; metro and tram cover the rest; airport to center is about 20 minutes on Metro Line 3/5.
